How SportSpot Works

1. Find Your Perfect Field

Search for a field using location and time, or search for a specific place by name and select a field from that location.

2. Choose Your Time

In the field page, you can update the date and time as needed. The page provides helpful reservation information including:

  • Minimum booking time
  • Blocked times
  • Allowed time patterns
  • Already reserved times

3. Make Your Reservation

Once you select a date and time, you'll need to:

  • Enter your name (must match the name used for payment)
  • Provide your phone number
  • Verify your phone number using an OTP code

4. Confirm Your Booking

After the initial reservation:

  • Your spot is temporarily reserved but not confirmed
  • Pay a 1 JOD reservation fee using Cliq (separate from field fees)
  • Complete payment within one hour to avoid automatic cancellation
  • Receive WhatsApp updates about your reservation status

5. Manage Your Reservations

Use the "Manage Reservation" link on the main page to:

  • View your active reservations
  • Check reservation status
  • Cancel reservations if needed
